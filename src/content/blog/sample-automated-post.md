## Understanding FHA Loan Limits in 2025

If you're a first-time buyer exploring [FHA financing](/loan-programs/fha-loan/), one of the first questions you'll hit is whether the home you want falls within FHA loan limits for your county.

### Why Loan Limits Matter

FHA loan limits cap how much you can borrow using an FHA-insured mortgage. Go above the limit and you'll need a [conventional loan](/loan-programs/conventional-loan/) or a [jumbo loan](/loan-programs/jumbo-loan/) — both of which typically require larger down payments and higher credit scores.

### What to Do If You're Over the Limit

If your target home exceeds the FHA limit for your county, you have options. Talk to our team about conventional alternatives that still offer competitive rates for first-time buyers.
